# Privacy Policy — Test-Agent Mobile App

**Last updated: 2026-06-21**

## Data Collection

The Test-Agent mobile app collects:
- **API key**: Stored securely in device Keychain (iOS) / EncryptedSharedPreferences (Android). Never transmitted to third parties.
- **Test commands**: Sent to your configured Test-Agent server. Not stored on device after submission.
- **Push notification token**: Used solely for test failure alerts. Stored by Firebase/APNs per their privacy policies.

## Data NOT Collected

- No personal identification information
- No location data
- No contacts or device identifiers
- No analytics or tracking

## Third-Party Services

- Firebase Cloud Messaging (Android push)
- Apple Push Notification Service (iOS push)

## Your Rights

- Delete all app data by uninstalling the app
- Contact: https://github.com/Wool-xing/Test-Agent/issues

## Changes

Updates to this policy will be posted in-app and on GitHub.
